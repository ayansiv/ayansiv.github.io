Meridian Advisory

Meridian Advisory supports EA-aligned organizations and mission-driven nonprofits. Many organizations hold meaningful reserves without the requisite infrastructure to manage them well. Meridian fills that gap.

The problem

Endowment consultants and OCIOs often only serve institutions with $250M or more in assets. Smaller nonprofits, giving funds, and research orgs are left to make reserve investment decisions ad hoc, through volunteer board members, or not at all. A $1M–$20M reserve portfolio still deserves a written investment policy, a deliberate allocation, and a defensible spend rule.

Good investment governance isn't just for large institutions.

Services include:

  • IPS drafting & governance documents
  • Asset allocation
  • Spend rate policy
  • Manager & fund selection
  • Annual review packages
  • Board education sessions

Approach

Meridian is advisory-only. Clients retain full control of their assets. EA-aligned organizations are served at subsidized or cost-recovery pricing. The goal is to make the kind of investment governance that large institutions take for granted accessible to the organizations that need it most.
