Meridian Advisory

Meridian Advisory provides investment consulting to EA-aligned organizations and mission-driven nonprofits. Most of these organizations hold meaningful reserves without the governance infrastructure to manage them well — no investment policy, no deliberate allocation, no spend rate framework. Meridian fills that gap, at pricing that reflects the mission.

The problem

Large endowment consultants serve institutions with $50M or more in assets. Smaller EA-aligned organizations — giving funds, infrastructure nonprofits, research orgs — are left to make reserve investment decisions ad hoc, through volunteer board members, or not at all. A $1M–$20M reserve portfolio still deserves a written investment policy, a deliberate allocation, and a defensible spend rule. Good investment governance isn't just for large institutions.

Services

  • Investment Policy Statement drafting. Governance documents defining investment objectives, risk tolerance, asset allocation ranges, and spending policy.
  • Asset allocation analysis. Custom allocation frameworks with Monte Carlo modeling to evaluate long-run sustainability across market scenarios.
  • Spend rate policy. Designing spend rules that balance liquidity needs against portfolio longevity, particularly for organizations with perpetual or long-horizon missions.
  • Manager & fund selection. Vetting external managers or funds consistent with investment policy and values alignment.
  • Annual review packages. Portfolio reporting, performance attribution, and policy compliance review formatted for board presentation.
  • Board education sessions. Structured programming on investment governance, fiduciary duty, and endowment management for nonprofit boards.

Approach

Meridian is advisory-only. No custody of assets, no discretionary management — clients retain full control. EA-aligned organizations are served at subsidized or cost-recovery pricing. The goal is to make the kind of investment governance that large institutions take for granted accessible to the organizations that need it most.
